Domestic Violence Survivors + Students

Studies have shown that children who witness domestic violence are twice as likely to abuse their own partners and children in the future. Your donation means that women who are overcoming abuse can receive counseling, financial support, and job training so that their children will have a brighter future.

Our students are chosen because they have the drive to succeed. We know that social guidance and financial support for these students will go a long way. Your donation can help change a life.

Different Ways to Give

  • COMPANY MATCHING GIFTS

    Does your employer have a matching gift program for your monetary donation and/or volunteer service? These programs can increase the impact of your donation to Of Home, Family and Future. Ask your Human Resources Department if these programs are available. Then contact info@ofhome.org for more information.

  • PAYROLL CAMPAIGNS

    Is there a giving campaign at your workplace? Please strongly consider designating your charitable contribution to Of Home, Family and Future.

  • PLANNED GIVING

    Please contact info@ofhome.org to discuss ways to give to Of Home, Family and Future.

  • SUPPORT US ON AMAZONSMILE

    For those of you who shop on Amazon.com, they have an exciting program called AmazonSmile. It’s a simple way for you to support your favorite charity every time you shop, at no cost to you. Once you choose your charity, Amazon automatically donates a small portion of the purchase price on eligible items whenever you place an order.

    If you’d like to designate Of Home, Family and Future as your charity of choice, which we certainly hope you will do, just go to the following link: http://smile.amazon.com/ch/20-2014390, sign into your Amazon account and then a confirmation should say “Your shopping will support Of Home Family and Future Inc” . It takes less than a minute!
